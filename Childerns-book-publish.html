Children's Book Publishing

American Book Publisher equips you with the necessary tools to assess our expertise; these are essential for achieving exceptional results.

Board Books

Designed for infants, these sturdy books are made from durable glossy paper, perfect for little ones who may chew on them. They feature vibrant characters and simple morals.

Picture Books

Aimed at children aged 3-5, these books offer slightly more detailed stories as kids in this age group have longer attention spans. Here, illustrations play a more dominant role than text.

Folklore

These books help children explore various aspects of life, promoting imagination and creativity. They illustrate the consequences of different actions, enriching the young reader's perspective.

Fairy Tales

Targeted at children aged 7 and up, fairy tales typically feature recurring characters such as giants, witches, and wizards. These stories make for excellent read-aloud experiences for children.
